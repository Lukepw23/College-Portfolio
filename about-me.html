Description
During my freshman year of high school, I was introduced to the wormhole of Marvel movies. I began watching the Iron Man movies and was instantly inspired by Tony Stark's digital assistant, JARVIS. It was at that moment I wondered how JARVIS functions and what would it take to make a version of him in the real world. I then began researching the art of programming and what I could accomplish by developing my skills in this space. I started this journey by enrolling in Computer Science and Programming classes at my school, and researching on my own what it would take for me to make my own version of JARVIS. Ever since that moment, I have been obsessed with developing my craft in programming while also creating my own digital assistant, A.D.A.
Last summer, I completed the University of Michigan's Joy of Coding course.
